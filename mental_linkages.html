Mental linkages

In the following activity you will find a set of terms and their definitions. Select the term and move it to the concept that corresponds.

If the concept background turns into blue, it means that the term has not been linked to a concept yet.

If the concept background turns yellow, it means that the term has been linked to a concept already. If you remove a term from the concept that was linked, it will be considered as an empty option when you select the process button.
